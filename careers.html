Current Positions

Venture associate positions are open for Fall 2016! Below is a description of this position. Visit our Learn page to find some relevant reading materials.

  • Source deals & pursue relevant opportunities with support from the General partners: frequently source deals from the Startup Shell, Dingman Center venture programs, and other relevant channels at the University. Review relevant opportunities with the General Partners.
  • Conduct company diligence with guidance from General Partners: in the event that a deal is pursued, a deep dive will be conducted with guidance from GPs. Associated are expected to contribute meaningfully to this process through additional research, meetings with industry professionals and potential customers, etc.
  • Conduct extensive market research: team members are expected to utilize tools such as VBIC, IBIS World, Hoovers, etc. to create comprehensive market reports to aid in the due diligence process.
  • Draft investment memorandums: thoroughly document investment opportunities the team is actively reviewing.
  • Provide support to ROV portfolio companies: connect Route One Ventures portfolio companies with investors, mentors, employees, and customers; help portfolio companies with strategic decisions, product roadmap, etc, either as a board member or informally.
  • Actively expand ROV network and update CRM: Seek and reach out to potential mentors, advisors, investors, and students to join the ROV network.
  • Contribute to Route One Ventures content: write, add, and improve the newsletter, master reading list, company blog, events, and any other Route One Ventures content
  • Opportunity to become a GP
  • Time commitment expected: 5-10 hours per week
    • Meeting every other week
    • Additional meetings on an as-needed basis
    • If you have any questions, reach out at contact@routeone.vc.
